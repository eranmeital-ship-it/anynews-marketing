Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) defines the boundaries of permitted use of AnyNews.ai. All users must comply with this policy in addition to the Terms of Service.

You may not use AnyNews.ai to generate, publish, or distribute content that is: defamatory or libelous; deliberately misleading or constituting disinformation; promoting violence, hatred, or discrimination; sexually explicit or exploitative; infringing on intellectual property rights; violating any applicable law or regulation.

3. Prohibited activities

You may not: attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, or extract source code from AnyNews.ai; use the service for spam, phishing, or social engineering; create accounts under false identities; resell access without an authorized partnership agreement; use automated tools to abuse the API beyond rate limits; attempt to circumvent security measures or access other users' data.

4. AI-generated content responsibility

Content generated by AnyNews.ai is produced by artificial intelligence. You are solely responsible for reviewing, editing, and approving all content before it is published. You accept liability for any content published through your connected social media channels.

5. Source attribution

AnyNews.ai attributes news sources when generating content. You must not remove or obscure source attributions in published content.

6. Enforcement

Violations may result in: a warning and request to correct the behavior; temporary suspension of your account; permanent termination without refund; reporting to relevant authorities where legally required.

7. Reporting violations

Report violations to abuse@anynews.ai. We investigate all reports within 48 hours.
