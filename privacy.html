Privacy Policy.

How we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

Last updated: May 17, 2026

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

Account registration data (name, email, password); billing information (processed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers); brand voice settings and custom instructions; social channel connection tokens (encrypted at rest).

Information collected automatically

Device information (browser, OS, screen resolution); usage data (features used, pages visited, session duration); IP address and approximate location; cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Cookie Policy).

Information from third parties

Social media profile data when you connect channels; payment confirmation from payment processors; analytics data from service providers.

2. How we use your information

We use your data to: provide and operate the AnyNews.ai service; generate and publish content on your behalf; process payments and manage your subscription; send service-related communications; improve and develop new features; ensure security and prevent fraud; comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process data under: contract performance (providing the service you signed up for); legitimate interests (improving the service, preventing fraud); consent (marketing emails — you can opt out); legal obligations (tax, regulatory compliance).

4. Data sharing

We share data only with: Service providers — hosting (AWS), payments (Stripe), analytics (privacy-focused), email delivery. Social platforms — when you connect channels, we transmit content you approve for publishing. Legal requirements — when compelled by law, subpoena, or court order. We never sell your personal data.

5. Data retention

Account data: retained while your account is active + 30 days after deletion. Published content data: retained for 12 months for analytics, then anonymized. Billing records: retained for 7 years per tax requirements. Server logs: retained for 90 days.

6. International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and European Union. For EU-to-US transfers, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses. See our Data Processing Agreement for details.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to: access, correct, or delete your data; restrict or object to processing; data portability; withdraw consent. See our GDPR Rights page for EU-specific rights. California residents: see CCPA section below.

8. CCPA disclosure (California)

California residents have the right to: know what personal information is collected; request deletion; opt out of sale (we do not sell data); non-discrimination for exercising rights. To exercise CCPA rights, email privacy@anynews.ai.

9. Children's privacy

AnyNews.ai is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have, we delete it promptly.

10. Security

We implement encryption (TLS 1.3, AES-256), access controls, regular audits, and incident response procedures. See our Security page for details.

11. Changes

Material changes are communicated via email 30 days before they take effect. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

Privacy inquiries: privacy@anynews.ai
Data Protection Officer: dpo@anynews.ai
