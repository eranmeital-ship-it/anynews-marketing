Cookie Policy.

Comprehensive information about our use of cookies.

Last updated: May 17, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, improve efficiency, and provide reporting information.

2. Strictly necessary cookies

These are essential for the website to function. They include: session cookies (maintaining your login); security cookies (CSRF protection); load-balancing cookies. You cannot opt out of strictly necessary cookies as the service cannot function without them.

3. Performance cookies

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors use AnyNews.ai. These cookies collect aggregated, anonymous data about: pages visited and time spent; features used; error occurrences; geographic region (country level). We do not use Google Analytics.

4. Functional cookies

These remember your preferences: language and region settings; theme preferences; dismissed notifications; onboarding progress.

5. Marketing cookies

We use minimal marketing cookies only for: measuring the effectiveness of our own advertising; attributing sign-ups to marketing campaigns. We do not serve third-party advertising on AnyNews.ai. We do not share cookie data with advertisers.

6. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by services we integrate with: Stripe (payment processing — fraud prevention); Social platforms (when using OAuth login). These are governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Cookie duration

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for the period specified (typically 30 days to 1 year) or until you delete them.

8. Managing cookies

You can manage cookies through: our cookie consent banner (shown on first visit); your browser settings (blocking or deleting cookies); your account settings (marketing preferences). Note that blocking certain cookies may affect functionality.

9. Updates

We update this policy when our cookie practices change. The "last updated" date reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Cookie-related inquiries: privacy@anynews.ai
