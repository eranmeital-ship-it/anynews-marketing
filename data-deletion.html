Data Deletion Request.

How to delete your AnyNews account and personal data.

Last updated: 21 May 2026
Effective date: 21 May 2026

Plain-English summary

How to get your data deleted from AnyNews. The short version:

  • Email info@anynews.ai with subject "Data Deletion Request" from your account email.
  • We confirm within 5 business days; complete deletion within 30 days.
  • We keep limited records (payment data for 7 years per Israeli tax law, security logs for 12 months) and then permanently delete.
  • Content already published to your social media accounts stays there; only AnyNews can delete what's on our servers.

How to request deletion

Email info@anynews.ai with:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • From: the email address on your AnyNews account (so we can verify the request is yours)
  • Include: any specific data you want removed beyond your account (such as specific posts, channels, or connected social account information)

What we delete

When we process your request, we delete:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All channels, posts, signals, and AI Output associated with your account
  • OAuth tokens for any connected social media accounts (this stops AnyNews from being able to publish on your behalf; it does not remove content already published)
  • Settings, preferences, and any other personal data tied to your account

What we retain (and why)

In some cases we are required to keep limited records after account deletion:

  • Payment records: 7 years from the transaction date (Israeli tax law)
  • Authentication and security logs: 12 months
  • Backups: purged within 90 days of account deletion

After these retention periods, all data is permanently deleted or fully anonymized. We do not retain anything else.

Timing

We will confirm receipt of your request within 5 business days, and complete the deletion within 30 days of receipt. If we cannot complete the deletion within 30 days (for legal or technical reasons), we will notify you with an explanation and a revised timeline.

Important to know

  • Content published to your social media accounts before deletion remains on those platforms. AnyNews cannot remove content from Instagram, Facebook, or other third-party platforms after publication. You must remove that content directly from each platform.
  • Cancellation vs deletion are different. Cancelling your subscription stops billing but keeps your account. Deletion removes everything.
  • Deletion is irreversible. Once we delete your data, we cannot restore it.

Need help?

If you have questions or want help with the process, email info@anynews.ai with subject "Privacy Request."
