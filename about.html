About us

Building the newsroom of the future.

AnyNews.ai was founded on a simple belief: the speed of news should not depend on the size of your team.

The problem.

A breaking story has a window. The first account to publish a credible, well-designed post captures the reach, the follows, and the authority. Everyone else is sharing yesterday's news.

But publishing fast requires a writer, a designer, a social manager, and a monitoring tool — a team most publishers cannot afford, and a workflow that takes hours even when they can.

The solution.

AnyNews is the first complete AI newsroom — a single app that monitors trusted sources in real time, detects breaking stories, and instantly generates written, designed, platform-optimized posts for every social network.

From signal to five published posts in under sixty seconds. No writer. No designer. No lag. Just speed, quality, and the authority that comes from being first.

What drives us

Our values.

Speed is everything

In news, second place is last place. Every feature we build is measured by how many seconds it saves.

Trust is non-negotiable

We only pull from verified sources. Every signal is scored. Credibility is the foundation — speed without trust is noise.

Built for creators

AnyNews is for the independent publisher, the niche expert, the one-person media operation that wants to compete with entire teams.
